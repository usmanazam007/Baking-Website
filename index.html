About Us

How Noors Kitchenette Work

Noor's Kitchenette is a home-based kitchenette that operates by taking orders from customers and preparing delicious baked goods right from the comfort of their own kitchen. They offer a variety of treats, from cakes and cookies to cupcakes and brownies. Customers can place their orders through phone calls, social media, or their website. Once the order is confirmed, Noor's Kitchenette bakes everything fresh and then arranges for pickup or delivery, depending on the customer's preference. It's a delightful way to enjoy homemade baked goods made with love.
